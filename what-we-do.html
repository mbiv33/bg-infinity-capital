What We Do

At BGIC, we invest in the fundamental building blocks of economic revitalization. Our approach centers on three core pillars that drive sustainable, community-first growth.

Real Estate Development

Transforming underutilized properties into commercial hubs, residential spaces, and industrial centers that support long-term economic growth.

Business Acquisition & Growth

Scaling businesses to drive returns while equipping Oklahomans with the skills, mentorship, and capital access needed to start, expand, and sustain successful enterprises.

Financial Innovation & Capital Deployment

Structuring investment models that attract long-term capital while maximizing economic participation for local communities.

Our Strategy

At BGIC, our strategy is built on sustainable growth, community impact, and long-term value creation. We focus on acquiring and developing high-potential real estate, transforming underutilized areas into thriving mixed-use spaces that attract businesses and residents while integrating smart infrastructure solutions.

Real Estate and Infrastructure Development

  • Acquiring strategic properties in underdeveloped but high-potential districts
  • Building mixed-use developments that attract businesses, create housing, and foster community engagement
  • Enhancing public spaces and integrating smart infrastructure solutions for modern, efficient urban growth

Small Business and Workforce Development

  • Creating business incubators to support new entrepreneurs and trade professionals
  • Partnering with technical schools, workforce programs, and industry leaders to create high-demand job training pipelines
  • Developing housing solutions that attract and retain skilled workers and entrepreneurs

Investment and Capital Growth

  • Deploying $50M+ in private capital, leveraging federal and local incentives
  • Ensuring investor security through asset-backed holdings and revenue-generating properties
  • Establishing long-term reinvestment strategies that turn initial capital into recurring economic benefits

Our Commitment

Success for BGIC is not just measured in returns on investment, but in economic vitality, workforce participation, and long-term community resilience. Our commitment includes:

Public-Private Collaboration

Working alongside state and municipal leaders, businesses, and civic organizations to foster economic renewal.

Market-Based Synergy

Leveraging public policy to develop opportunistic pathways where effort and contribution determine success, rather than external mandates.

Sustainable Growth Models

Implementing long-term investment strategies that build lasting economic stability in communities.
