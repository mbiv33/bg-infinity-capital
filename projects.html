Our Projects

Flagship Initiative

The Prairie Flywheel Initiative

The Prairie Flywheel Initiative embodies this commitment, channeling the resilience, ingenuity, and entrepreneurial spirit of Oklahomans to build a dynamic, self-sustaining economic ecosystem.

Designed to create lasting economic momentum, the initiative strategically aligns investment, workforce development, and infrastructure expansion to generate continuous cycles of job creation, business growth, and urban revitalization. More than just development, this is about empowering people—ensuring that every hardworking individual has access to the opportunities, resources, and networks needed to succeed.

This initiative will set a new benchmark for economic renewal, positioning Oklahoma City as a national model for sustainable, inclusive growth that benefits all.

We are Passionate.

We are Passionate, Because It's Personal

Economics has always started and ended with people. For Marcus Bivines, CEO of BGIC, it's personal. Growing up in under-resourced neighborhoods of OKC in the '90s, he saw firsthand what it means to have no economic prospects, no financial agency, and no clear path forward. Determined to spark lasting change, he founded the Prairie Flywheel Initiative—a bold effort to ignite economic renewal through geographic revitalization, workforce empowerment, and business incubation.

A Smarter Approach to Economic Growth

At BGIC, we know that overlooked communities don't need abstract policies—they need practical, investment-backed solutions rooted in their strengths. The Prairie Flywheel Initiative is a comprehensive, strategic effort to unlock the economic potential of these regions—aligning capital, infrastructure, and human resources to fuel sustained growth.

By leveraging Opportunity Zones, New Market Tax Credits (NMTC), and Tax Increment Financing (TIF), we will maximize private and public funding to create lasting, self-sustaining opportunities.

With a strong alliance of partners and champions, this initiative is more than an investment—it's a movement. The Prairie Flywheel Initiative is setting a new standard for economic renewal in Oklahoma City and beyond.

The Prairie Flywheel Initiative is more than an economic strategy—it's a call to action. Invest, partner, or join us in shaping Oklahoma's economic future. Together, we can create lasting change that fuels generational wealth, strengthens communities, and drives true economic mobility.
