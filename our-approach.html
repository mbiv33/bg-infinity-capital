Our Approach

At BGIC, we believe that transformational economic development requires a strategic, values-driven approach that fosters long-term growth, equity, and innovation. Our methodology is built on a three-pillar framework that ensures capital, opportunity, and vision align to create lasting impact.

CRED Community-Rooted, Economically-Driven

We are CRED – Community-Rooted, Economically-Driven

Our Community-Rooted, Economically-Driven (CRED) approach ensures that urban communities don't just grow—they thrive. We foster a shared vision of progress and innovation, prioritizing investment in people, places, and potential to drive self-sustaining economic ecosystems.

By engaging local voices, aligning resources with community needs, and fostering economic synergy, we ensure that growth is gained and sustained locally. Our approach bridges the gap between economic opportunity and grassroots empowerment.

COP Capital for Good

We use COP – Capital for Good

At BGIC, we believe capital is a powerful tool for transformation, not just profit. We deploy investments that generate strong financial returns while creating meaningful social and economic impact, ensuring that wealth-building opportunities reach historically overlooked communities.

By leveraging innovative financing models and aligning private and public capital, we drive sustainable economic participation, business expansion, and long-term community resilience.

BOS Blue Ocean Strategy

We know BOS – Blue Ocean Strategy

We don't just compete—we create. BGIC operates under a Blue Ocean Strategy, seeking high-impact, low-competition opportunities that redefine markets rather than fighting for space in existing ones. We identify businesses that generate new demand, unlock untapped market potential, and disrupt outdated economic models to build sustainable, scalable enterprises.

By investing in visionary companies, emerging industries, and transformative real estate, we position Oklahoma City as a hub for next-generation economic development.
