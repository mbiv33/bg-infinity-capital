Urban Investment & Redevelopment Executive Summary

Invest. Develop. Transform.

BG Infinity Capital (BGIC) is a hybrid development and investment firm dedicated to creating thriving economic centers through strategic urban redevelopment and financial innovation. By combining the strategic drive of an investment group with the expertise of a development firm, BGIC fosters economic growth by unlocking the potential of underutilized assets and resources.

Our Mission

BGIC is committed to building strong, self-sustaining communities by focusing on community-rooted economic development. We leverage innovative financing structures, strategic partnerships, and development expertise to unlock the full potential of urban development and renewal projects. Our goal is to unlock the potential of overlooked regions by investing in infrastructure, supporting local businesses, and fostering an environment where hard work and ingenuity lead to long-term prosperity.

We strive to:

Strengthen Local Economies

Focus on regional revitalization that ensures Oklahomans in underserved areas can actively participate in economic growth.

Encourage Hard Work & Innovation

Champion the entrepreneurial spirit and resilience of Oklahomans by creating opportunities where effort and ingenuity are rewarded.

Redefine Development & Investment

Move beyond traditional models that perpetuate inequality, instead fostering public-private partnerships (PPPs) that generate lasting economic and social impact.

What We Do

At BGIC, we drive economic transformation by investing in the core elements that fuel sustainable growth. Through strategic real estate development, we revitalize underutilized properties into thriving commercial, residential, and industrial spaces that anchor long-term prosperity. Our business acquisition and growth initiatives empower entrepreneurs with the tools, mentorship, and capital needed to scale successful enterprises, strengthening local economies. By leveraging financial innovation and strategic capital deployment, we create investment models that attract long-term funding while ensuring inclusive economic participation. At BGIC, we don't just invest—we build lasting opportunities for communities to thrive.
